Addiction Treatment

Our team has over a decade of experience specializing in the medical needs of those in addiction recovery. We recognize that the disease of addiction is complex and multi-faceted and understand that there is not a one-size-fits-all approach to treatment.

Medication-Assisted Treatment

Whole Health believes that medication-assisted treatment can be a highly effective option for managing addiction recovery. We also believe in the importance of having qualified physicians monitor the progress of each client who receives this level of treatment.

A complete medication-assisted treatment plan includes holistic therapy modalities such as individual and group behavioral therapy sessions. This comprehensive approach simultaneously addresses the physical and psychological effects of addiction.

Comorbid Diseases

Comorbid diseases, or co-occurring disorders, are conditions when addiction is present alongside a mental health or medical disorder. Studies show that nearly half of those with an addiction also have a mental health disorder and vice versa.
